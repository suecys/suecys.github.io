About me

Enthusiastic learner and innovative thinker, Youngsoo Choi is currently the business analyst for the PI project of Hyundai Merchant Marine, leading the construction and development of the company's booking & documentation system. With 8 years of experience in the shipping industry and her personal interest in IT, she is not only able to create business processes, but also able to understand and build systems.

She has worked in the customer service department for a total of five years. She has a customer service mindset, which helps her while building the new system. She believes that a good system should be an embodiment of the business process itself. She is always interested in new things, and does not stop to improve herself through education.
